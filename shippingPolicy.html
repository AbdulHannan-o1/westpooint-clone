Shipping Policy

The estimated delivery time for Karachi is 2 to 3 working days.


The estimated delivery time for rest of Pakistan is 4 to 8 working days (depending on the destination).


We at Westpoint Pakistan aim to process and dispatch your order within 12 hours once the order is confirmed


You are requested to be available on the provided contact number so our team can reach you.


Timely delivery is subject to availability of stock, courier load, weather conditions, political disturbances, and other unforeseen situations. However, we try our best to provide all necessary support in case an order is delayed by any unforeseen circumstances.
